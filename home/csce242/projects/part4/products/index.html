Whiskey

Whiskey is a distilled alcoholic beverage made from fermented grain mash, aged in wooden casks, and known for its diverse flavors, often including notes of caramel, vanilla, and smokiness.

Vodka

Vodka is a distilled alcoholic beverage typically made from fermented grains or potatoes, known for its neutral flavor profile and high alcohol content.

Rum

Rum is a distilled alcoholic beverage made from sugarcane byproducts, typically aged in barrels, known for its rich and diverse flavor profile ranging from sweet and spiced to robust and complex.

Tequila

Tequila is a distilled spirit made from the fermented juice of the blue agave plant, offering a distinctive and robust flavor profile with notes of earthy agave, citrus, and a touch of peppery spice.
